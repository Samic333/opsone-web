HIGH-LEVEL PRODUCT VISION
==================================================

This system has two main products:

A. OpsOne Web Platform
A multi-tenant aviation operations SaaS platform used by the service provider and airline customers.

B. CrewAssist iPad App
The operational mobile/iPad companion app used by pilots, cabin crew, engineers, and selected airline staff depending on permissions/modules.

- Airline workspaces manage airline operations, personnel, documents, notices, roster-related tools, safety, training, etc.
- CrewAssist must align with the web-side permissions/modules and remain the only active iPad app
